About NewlyPracticing

NewlyPracticing is a non-profit initiative created by the Al-Madini Foundation to provide much needed support for new muslims and born-muslims who would like to change their lives around and follow Islam the way it was intended to be followed.

The service provides: Essential information without overwhelming you; a loving community to nurture you along your journey; challenges with rewards to help you visualise milestones; one-to-one advice from our students of knowledge who can help target your specific needs; articles in our blog to educate and help you relate to others who have been on your journey
